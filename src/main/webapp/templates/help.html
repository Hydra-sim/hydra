Simulations:

In the simulations tab you can see a list of all previous simulation. Click on simulation name to edit simulation. You can also delete, compare and password protect your simulations.

Timetables:

In the timetables tab you can see a list of all timetables . Click on timetable name to edit timetable. You can also create new timetables and delete them..

Presets:

In the presets tab you can see a list of all presets. A preset is a pre-defined simulation that you can rub as is or edit to cuztomise it. Click on preset name to edit it. You can delete presets, but also save a new simulation as a preset.
